Responsible gaming

Real-money games should always remain a form of entertainment, not a way to fix financial problems or earn a stable income.

Before you join Stake or any similar platform, decide in advance how much money you are comfortable risking and treat that amount as the cost of entertainment. Never play with funds needed for rent, bills, debts or other essential expenses.


Avoid chasing losses or trying to “win it back”. Take regular breaks, and stop immediately if you feel frustrated, angry or out of control. If you notice that gaming starts to affect your relationships, work or mental health, seek professional help and consider using self-exclusion or limit tools.


Stake provides its own tools and information for responsible gaming on the official website. Please read those resources carefully and use the platform only if you can maintain a healthy, balanced approach.
